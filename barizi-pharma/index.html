About Us

Practical Pharmacy Training for a Changing Healthcare Sector

Pharma Excellence Academy is a professional pharmacy training institution dedicated to excellence in pharmaceutical education, healthcare entrepreneurship, and clinical practice training.

Our programs combine theory, practical exposure, mentorship, and real-world pharmaceutical experience to prepare competent, innovative, and competitive professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

Who can apply?

Anyone interested in healthcare, pharmacy, or pharmaceutical business can apply.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes. We offer physical, online, and hybrid learning options.

How long are the courses?

Course duration varies by program, generally ranging from 12 weeks to 14 weeks.

Are practical sessions included?

Yes. Our training includes practical and hands-on learning experiences.

Do students receive certificates?

Yes. Certificates are awarded upon successful completion of courses.

Can I study while working?

Yes. Flexible schedules include weekend and online classes.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Flexible payment options are available for some courses.

Can international students apply?

Yes. International students are welcome for selected programs.
